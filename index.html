About Green Haven

Green Haven is a boutique plant shop located in the heart of Grand Rapids. We specialize in carefully selected indoor plants that thrive in Michigan's climate.

Our mission is to help you bring the beauty and benefits of nature into your living and working spaces with plants that are both beautiful and easy to care for.

Each plant in our shop is hand-picked for quality and comes with care instructions to ensure it thrives in your home.
